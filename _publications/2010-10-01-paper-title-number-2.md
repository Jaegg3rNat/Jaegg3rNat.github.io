excerpt: 'We study the role of multiplicative stochastic processes in the description of the dynamics of an order parameter near a critical point. We show that, even though the position of the fixed point depends on the stochastic prescription, the critical exponents do not. Therefore, different dynamics driven by different stochastic prescriptions (such as Itô, Stratonovich, anti-Itô and so on) are in the same universality class.'
